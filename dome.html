Technical Specifications

Materials + Systems

🏗 Structure

  • Earthbag or compressed earth block shell
  • ~32 ft diameter · 800 SF living level
  • 2–4 ft composted soil burial with living roof
  • Juniper timber: interior accents, shelving, door frames
  • Rammed earth retaining walls for buried roof
  • Cob + earthen plaster interior finish · breathable
  • Adobe knee walls on greenhouse

🌡 Thermal

  • Passive thermal mass holds 55–65°F year-round
  • High diurnal swings (40°F+) absorbed by buried mass
  • Rocket mass heater · juniper fuel · ultra-efficient
  • Earth tube ventilation · small diameter · filtered
  • South greenhouse as solar heat battery in winter
  • Adjustable vents manage greenhouse → dome airflow

💧 Water

  • Cistern fed by greenhouse roof + external catchment
  • Thermosiphon hot water · south slope metal panel
  • Aquaponics DC pump · solar powered
  • Graywater → greenhouse → orchard swales
  • Blackwater → composting toilet vault
  • Olla floor irrigation in herb zones

⚡ Energy

  • 2–4 kW off-grid solar array + battery bank
  • Direct DC circuits for pumps + fans
  • Solar dehydrator in utility alcove
  • Solar oven (external south-facing)
  • Hand-crank or plunger-barrel washer
  • No dependency on grid · redundancy built in

🌱 Indoor Growing

  • Substrate floors: gravel → sand → compost → pavers
  • Moisture channels connect to aquaponics sump
  • Shallow herbs, greens, mushrooms, microgreens in floor
  • Kitchen herb growbed above fish tanks
  • Greenhouse: berries, dwarf citrus, figs, tomatoes
  • Removable wood slats allow floor access

💰 Cost Estimate

  • Earthbag shell + airform: $18–25k
  • Concrete + labor (WWOOF): $8–12k
  • Utilities rough-in: $4–6k
  • Interior finish (cob, plaster, floor): $3–5k
  • Composted soil burial (on-site): ~$0
  • Aquaponics system: $2–4k
  • Total: $35–52k · primary use of John's $45k
Build Philosophy
